Welcome to Taskpaper Go!

Taskpaper Go! is a responsive web app that allows you to view your taskpaper files in any web browser or on your smartphone. Just click the button below to connect Taskpaper Go! to your Dropbox account and drop your files into the freshly created Taskpaper Go! folder.

Privacy: Taskpaper Go! transfers your personal data directly between your browser and Dropbox. No data is sent to or stored on any other servers. Taskpaper Go! is open source and released under the MIT License. Feel free to check out the source code on GitHub.
